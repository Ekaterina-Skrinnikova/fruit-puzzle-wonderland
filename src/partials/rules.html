How to Play

  • 1

    Swap adjacent fruits to form a row or column of three or more identical fruits

  • 2

    Match four or more fruits to create special fruits with powerful effects

  • 3

    Clear obstacles like ice, vines, and more by matching nearby fruits

  • 4

    Complete levels by collecting specific fruit types or reaching score targets

  • 5

    Use special boosters to overcome tricky levels and advance faster

  • 6

    Progress through the vibrant map, unlocking new levels and challenges as you go
